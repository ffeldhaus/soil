Welcome to the Soil Game! An Interactive Learning Experience

Soil is an interactive simulation game focused on agriculture. As a player, you'll make critical ecological and economic decisions, experiencing their direct impacts on your virtual farm.

This game aims to teach the principles of sustainable farming, resource management, and the interplay between agricultural practices and environmental health.

How to Play

To participate in a game session, you will typically receive login credentials (a game ID, player number, and password) from your game administrator or teacher. Use these to log in.

If you are a teacher or group leader interested in hosting a game, you'll need to register as an Administrator. Once registered, you can create and manage your own game sessions for your students or participants.
